Consider the one dimensional Euler equations in the primitive variable formulation:

\begin{align}
    \deldt \W + A(\W) \deldx \W = 0 &&
\end{align}

with the primitive state vector $\W$ and Jacobi matrix $A(\W)$

\begin{align}
    \W = \begin{pmatrix}
          \rho \\ v \\ p
         \end{pmatrix}
&&
    A(\W) = \frac{\del \F(\W)}{\del \W} = 
            \begin{pmatrix}
              v & \rho & 0 \\
              0 & v & 1/\rho \\
              0 & \rho c_s^2 & v
             \end{pmatrix}
\end{align}
